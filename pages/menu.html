TASTY MENU

NOODLES

  • emi.

    Char siu pork, springy noodles, and a rich, velvety tonkotsu broth. Crisp bean sprouts add freshness, while wood ear mushrooms provide an earthy depth. A perfect balance of umami and texture in every bite.

  • yuki.

    Spicy kimchi, savory ground pork, and chewy noodles. Sweet corn kernels offer bursts of flavor, while a runny egg yolk adds luxurious richness. A bold and satisfying fusion of Korean and Japanese tastes.

  • aiko.

    Smoky grilled chicken, silky spinach, and firm noodles in a light yuzu-infused broth. Crunchy bamboo shoots contrast with melt-in-your-mouth roasted garlic. A refreshing yet comforting bowl that delights the senses.

  • sakura.

    Shrimp, bouncy noodles, and a creamy miso broth. Crispy nori sheets provide umami, while pickled ginger adds zesty brightness. A seafood lover's dream with layers of complementary flavors.

  • mei.

    Braised beef, hearty udon noodles, and a aromatic shoyu broth. Crisp snow peas bring a fresh crunch, while shiitake mushrooms add earthy notes. A robust and satisfying bowl that warms from the inside out.

  • rin.

    Marinated tofu, thin noodles, and a fragrant vegetable broth. Roasted butternut squash adds sweetness, while crispy kale chips provide a nutrient-rich crunch. A colorful and nourishing plant-based delight.

DRINKS

  • kaori.

    Smooth vodka, tangy lime juice, and a splash of ginger beer. Fresh mint leaves add a cooling touch, while candied ginger brings a spicy-sweet kick. A refreshing blend of zesty and aromatic flavors in every sip.

  • akira.

    Crisp apple juice, tart cranberry, and sparkling water. A cinnamon stick infuses warmth, while a frozen grape garnish adds a playful, icy touch. A delightful non-alcoholic option bursting with fruity notes.

  • nao.

    Premium junmai, subtle lychee liqueur, and a hint of rose water. Delicate sakura petals float on top, while a twist of lemon peel adds citrusy brightness. An elegant and balanced fusion of Japanese and floral elements.

  • hana.

    Smoky Japanese whisky, sweet vermouth, and a dash of bitters. A crystal-clear ice sphere melts slowly, while a brandied cherry adds a luxurious finish. A sophisticated sipper with layers of complex flavors.

  • saki.

    Smooth rum, creamy coconut milk, and fresh pineapple juice. Crushed ice keeps it frosty, while a sprinkle of toasted coconut adds textural contrast. A vacation in a glass, transporting you to sun-soaked beaches.

  • miyu.

    Tea-infused cocktail marries fragrant green tea, citrusy yuzu juice, and a touch of honey syrup. Muddled shiso leaves provide an herbal note, while a spritz of soda water adds effervescence. A light and invigorating drink.
